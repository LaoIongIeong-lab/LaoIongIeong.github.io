- AI for health and mental health.

Calmness is a crucial component of health. I practice mindfulness and understand the value of being focused, which I think is equivalent to calmness. Nowadays, people seem to be more anxious and depressed than ever. It would be truly helpful to address this issue either by incorporating calming elements into technological systems or by alternative means.

My envisioned strategies for promoting calmness could be categorized as follows:
1. Prevention: This can involve building a calming environment by filtering anxious information.
2. Prediction: Such a system can predict early individuals who are likely to develop anxiety or depression.
3. Guided learning about meta-awareness: This may involve teaching mindfulness through novel human-computer interactions or educational technology that can teach users to identify distraction at a meta level.
4. Intervention: Medicine (e.g., Agarwood), or BCIs such as BrainCo/強腦科技.

I believe that potential solutions may exist to address the different dimensions of mental health challenges -- thereby alleviating, if not freeing us from, the inherent constraints in human affective regulation.
